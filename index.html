How does the game work?
Matchstick Plus is a puzzle game where you move matchsticks to create new shapes and solve equations. Each level presents a challenge that requires strategic thinking and creativity.
Which devices are supported?
We support iOS and Android devices. macOS and Windows support will be available soon.
Is my progress saved?
Yes, your game progress is automatically saved locally on your device. You can continue from where you left off anytime.
How can I get hints for difficult puzzles?
You can use hints by tapping the lightbulb icon. Free users get limited hints, while premium users have unlimited access to hints.
Are there different difficulty levels?
Yes! The game features multiple difficulty levels from beginner to expert. Each level offers unique challenges and puzzle types.
